At Diypowertools ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting and respecting your

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services, including:

your name, email address, and other contact details.
Usage Data: We automatically collect information about how you access and use our website, such
as IP addresses, browser type, operating system, and pages visited. This is done using cookies and
similar tracking technologies.
Affiliate Data: If you make a purchase via an affiliate link on our website or social media, we
may collect information regarding the transaction, including purchase amount, products, and time
of purchase.
2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

To send you newsletters, product recommendations, or promotional materials if you've opted-in

To track affiliate link transactions and offer product recommendations.
To comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

We partner with third-party affiliates to provide affiliate links and product recommendations. When

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
We use cookies to improve your user experience and analyze web traffic. Cookies are small data

manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

We may share your personal information in the following circumstances:

7. Your Rights and Choices
Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, update, or delete the personal
information we hold about you. If you'd like to exercise these rights, please contact us via the contact
details below.
8. Children's Privacy
Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal
